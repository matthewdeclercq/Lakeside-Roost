Getting There

  1. Navigate to the driveway entrance.
  2. On the lefthand side of the road there is a sign on a tree that says “Declercq” — turn left into that driveway.
  3. You will pass our neighbor's camp on the left. Smile and wave, but keep-er-moving. There will be red and white streamers tied to the trees once you get to the property line.
  4. Keep following the red and white streamers until you get to the campsite.

NOTE: Download the entire map area for offline use before you get here. Verizon is the only carrier that works at Lake Medora — once you are NE of the Lake, there is no cell service at all.
